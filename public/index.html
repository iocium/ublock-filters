lists.malwarewarden.org

Adblock Plus–compatible lists by iocium to block annoyances and bypass paywalls

Available Lists

  • Annoyances List: annoyances.txt
  • Paywall Bypass List: paywall.txt

How to Use

uBlock Origin

  1. Install uBlock Origin from your browser’s extension store.
  2. Open the uBlock Origin dashboard by clicking the icon and selecting the settings (⚙️) icon.
  3. Navigate to the Filter Lists tab.
  4. Scroll to the Custom section and click Import… (or “Import… from URL”).
  5. Paste the URL of the desired list:
    https://lists.malwarewarden.org/annoyances.txt
    or
    https://lists.malwarewarden.org/paywall.txt
  6. Click Apply changes to add the filter.

Adblock Plus

  1. Install Adblock Plus from your browser’s extension store.
  2. Click the Adblock Plus icon and select Options.
  3. Go to the Advanced → Add your own filters section.
  4. Paste one of the following URLs into the textbox:
    https://lists.malwarewarden.org/annoyances.txt
    or
    https://lists.malwarewarden.org/paywall.txt
  5. Click Add filter subscription (or “Apply/Save”) to subscribe.
